How to Take Part

Taking part is easy, just complete the steps below and you'll be ready to start logging your data.

1. Take our questionnaire

2. Fill in our Get Started form - you will only have to do this once, and then you can start logging in data.

3. Start recording your water cuts by clicking on "Log your data".

Whenever you experience a break in service, simply go to our home page, click "Log your data", and let us know about it.

Reporting any breaks in services will just take a couple of minutes each day, the hard part is remembering to do it!
