Log Data

This page can be used to log your water and power supply performances over the day. Please visit our take part page before logging data!

Press the appropriate buttons for both water and for electricity service performance. If there was a period of less than normal performance, tell us at what time you noticed it, and at what time (if at all) it came back.

Water Service

If you've experienced no interruptions of water supply since your last log, press the green button.

If you've experienced a period of reduction in the water pressure or quality, press the orange button.

If you've experienced a period of no water at all, press the red button.

Electricity Service

If you've experienced no breaks in your power supply since your last log, press the green button.

If you've experienced an electricy cut from the municipality electricty, but you own a private supply of power (such as a generator), press the orange button

If you've experienced an electricty cut from the municipality electricity, and you don't own a generator, press the red button.
