Why Get Involved

Your support is vital to us achieving our ambition of mapping the water and power supply networks, but that's not the only reason you should volunteer with us.

Let us tell you why you should take advantage of the opportunity to do some good.

Be good to others, and yourself

By volunteering to become a citizen scientist, you're not just helping us with our scientific research, you're also helping the people that is research is about.

People who boil their water before consumption to avoid being ill. People who avoid showering because they want to save water, just in case.

Still not convinced? Studies show that altruistic behaviour promotes well-being, health and longevity. Worth a shot?

Be part of the scientific community

We appreciate every volunteer. That's why we will personally recognise your contribution in our upcoming scientific journal publications about the research behind Networking Water, as a very special thank you!

Make a difference!

Networking Water is in the business of changing the way we approach research. By taking part in our Volunteer Organization, you can help generate valuable data that will not only help us as researchers, but also demonstrates to the scientific community that crowdsourced information can make a significant contribution to science.
