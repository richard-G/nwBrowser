Privacy Policy

Here at Networking Water we take your privacy very seriously. This notice will tell you how we are going to use the data you send to us.

Who are we?

We're Networking Water, a research group created by a team of researchers from Civil Engineering's Centre for Doctoral Training at Imperial College London.
Our goals are strictly that of a non-profit initiative meant only to advance academic research.

What do we do?

We are in the business of changing the way we approach research on the water supply. To do this, we need to collect vast amounts of data in areas where little or no information about the water supply currently exist.
That's where you come in. The data you collect for us will be used to formulate a model for the water supply in your area.

Personal Information

The only personal information we require from you is your location, and this will be used for the sole purpose of our research. This information will be kept only for as long as necessary and will be kept anonymous.

If you have any questions about our handling of personal information, the best way to contact us is through our facebook page, which can be found on our "Contact Us" page.
